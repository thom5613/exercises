About Us

Kiku Bistro is a locally-owned Asian restaurant featuring innovative sushi rolls and traditional stir-fries, teriyakis, and tempuras. We feature fare for the experienced sushi aficionado, as well as the guest seeking to explore new cuisine. Come visit us in the heart of Uptown for our daily Happy Hour and stay for dinner and our new and expanded dinner menu. We look forward to meeting you!
